Chocolate Cake

A delicious and rich chocolate cake recipe perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the sugar and butter.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well with each addition.
  4. Mix in the flour, milk, and cocoa powder until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into a greased and floured 9x9 inch pan.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven.
  7. Allow to cool.
